Presentation#

Greg. (alias Grégoire) - I am a Solution Architect at AWS WWPS. My role involves assisting customers in understanding technologies, adopting products, integrating new processes, and transforming their organizations. I specialize in crafting solutions based on Cloud, Modern Applications, and Automation technologies, leveraging both the Cloud stack and the spirit of Open Source.

I thrive on building relationships with decision-makers and serving as the customer’s technical point of contact. Educating prospects on product implementation and usage is among my responsibilities. Fueled by the Open Source mindset, I have actively participated in various projects involving Cloud Solutions, OpenShift (Kubernetes), Docker, Ansible, Java/NodeJS/Python, Business Automation, and Rule Engine technologies. Prior to my current role, I served as a Senior Cloud Architect at Red Hat for more than a decade and held the position of Tech Leader at Capgemini for several years.

Some kinds of business engagement I have already done:

  • Customer meeting for business, sales, projects and deliveries topics.
  • Transformation Project, Integration Project, Dev Project.
  • Cloud Architecture, Application Design, Integration Topic, ADR, Cloud patterns.
  • Get feedbacks from customer product adoption and product success/pain
  • Team planning, Tech leader,
  • Coding, Continuous integration,
  • Knowledge transfer, user support, customer training or evangelist.
